About Us

Get to know the team behind SoftCodec and our mission to innovate the future

Who We Are

SoftCodec is a premier software development company dedicated to creating innovative digital solutions that drive business success. Founded in 2015, we've grown from a small team of passionate developers to a full-service technology partner for businesses worldwide.

Our team combines technical expertise with creative thinking to deliver custom software solutions that solve complex business challenges. We believe in building long-term partnerships with our clients, understanding their unique needs, and delivering solutions that exceed expectations.

With a focus on quality, innovation, and client satisfaction, SoftCodec has established itself as a trusted name in the software development industry.

Our Mission

To empower businesses through innovative technology solutions that drive growth, efficiency, and competitive advantage.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in creating transformative digital experiences that shape the future of industries worldwide.

Our Values

  • Excellence - We strive for the highest quality in everything we do
  • Integrity - We operate with honesty and transparency
  • Collaboration - We believe in the power of teamwork

Our Leadership

Salman Shoukat

CEO & Founder

With over 3 years of experience in software development and business leadership, Salman Shoukat has established SoftCodec as a leading technology solutions provider. His visionary approach and deep technical expertise have guided our company through significant growth and innovation.

Salman's leadership philosophy centers on fostering creativity, embracing cutting-edge technologies, and building lasting client relationships. Under his guidance, SoftCodec has successfully delivered over 30 projects across various industries, earning recognition for excellence in software development.

Before founding SoftCodec, Salman held leadership positions at several tech giants where he honed his skills in strategic planning and team management. He holds a Master's degree in Computer Science and is passionate about mentoring the next generation of tech leaders.

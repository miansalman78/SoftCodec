Our Application Process

What to expect when you apply for a position at SoftCodec

1. Application

Submit your resume and cover letter through our online application system.

2. Initial Screening

Our HR team will review your application and conduct a brief phone interview.

3. Technical Assessment

Complete a technical assessment or coding challenge relevant to the position.

4. Team Interview

Meet with the team you'll be working with to discuss your experience and approach.

5. Final Interview

A final interview with senior management to discuss your fit with the company.

6. Offer

If successful, you'll receive a job offer with details about compensation and benefits.
